Frequently Asked Questions

How do I adopt a pet?

Start by browsing our available pets, then fill out an adoption application. Our team will review your application and contact you within 48 hours.

What are the adoption fees?

Adoption fees vary by animal and age, typically ranging from $50-$300. This covers spaying/neutering, vaccinations, and microchipping.

Can I volunteer if I'm under 18?

Yes! Volunteers aged 14-17 can participate with parental consent. We have special youth volunteer programs available.

Do you accept owner surrenders?

We accept owner surrenders by appointment only and based on available space. Please contact us to discuss your situation.

How can I donate supplies?

We accept donations at all our locations during business hours. Check our wishlist for most needed items.

Do you offer training classes?

Yes, we offer basic obedience and puppy training classes. Visit our events page for schedules and registration.
