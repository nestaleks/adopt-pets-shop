Frequently Asked Questions

What are the adoption fees?

Adoption fees vary depending on the pet's age and species. Dogs typically range from $150-$300, cats from $100-$200. The fee covers spaying/neutering, vaccinations, and microchipping.

How long does the adoption process take?

The adoption process typically takes 2-5 days from application to taking your pet home. This includes application review, meet-and-greet scheduling, and final approval.

What do I need to bring for adoption?

Please bring a valid ID, proof of residence, and landlord approval if you're renting. If you have other pets, bring their vaccination records. We also recommend bringing a carrier for cats or a leash for dogs.

Can I return a pet if it doesn't work out?

We understand that sometimes adoptions don't work out as planned. We have a 30-day return policy where you can bring the pet back to us. We'll work with you to find a better match or provide resources to help resolve any issues.

Do you offer post-adoption support?

Yes! We provide lifetime support for all adopted pets. This includes behavioral advice, training resources, and veterinary referrals. We're always here to help ensure a successful adoption.
