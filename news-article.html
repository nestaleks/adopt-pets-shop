Spring Adoption Fair - March 15-16: Find Your Perfect Companion

Sarah Johnson

Events Coordinator

Join us for two days of fun and find your new best friend!

Mark your calendars! Our biggest adoption event of the year is coming this March 15-16. Join us for two incredible days filled with adoptable pets, family activities, and the chance to find your perfect companion.

What to Expect

The Spring Adoption Fair is more than just an adoption event ‚Äì it's a celebration of the human-animal bond. This year, we're expecting over 100 adoptable pets, including dogs, cats, rabbits, and even some birds. Each animal will be ready to meet potential families and find their forever homes.

Our dedicated staff and volunteers will be on hand to help match you with the perfect pet for your lifestyle. Whether you're looking for an energetic jogging companion, a lap cat, or a gentle friend for your children, we'll help you find your ideal match.

Special Features This Year

  • 50% off adoption fees for all pets over 3 years old
  • Free starter kits for first-time pet owners
  • Professional pet photography - get a free portrait with your new pet
  • Training demonstrations every hour
  • Veterinary consultations available on-site

Our amazing volunteers will be there to help you find your perfect match

Family Activities

Even if you're not ready to adopt, there's plenty to enjoy! We'll have face painting, a petting zoo, food trucks, and live music throughout both days. Kids can participate in our "Junior Pet Care" workshop, where they'll learn about responsible pet ownership through fun, interactive activities.

"Last year's Spring Adoption Fair was incredible ‚Äì we found homes for 87 pets in just two days! This year, we're hoping to break that record." - Dr. Michael Chen, Shelter Director

Preparation Tips for Potential Adopters

If you're planning to adopt at the fair, here are some tips to make the process smoother:

  1. Bring a valid ID and proof of residence
  2. If you rent your home, bring proof that pets are allowed
  3. Consider pre-filling our adoption application online
  4. Bring the whole family ‚Äì everyone should meet the new pet
  5. If you have other pets, bring a recent photo to show our adoption counselors

COVID-19 Safety Measures

Your safety is our priority. We'll have hand sanitizing stations throughout the venue, and our staff will follow all recommended health guidelines. The event will be held primarily outdoors with plenty of space for social distancing.

Can't Make It to the Fair?

If you can't attend the Spring Adoption Fair, don't worry! You can still help by:

  • Sharing this event on social media
  • Making a donation to support our adoption programs
  • Volunteering at future events
  • Visiting our shelter during regular hours

We can't wait to see you at the Spring Adoption Fair! Together, we can make a difference in the lives of homeless pets and help them find the loving families they deserve.
