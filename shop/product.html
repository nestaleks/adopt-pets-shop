Product Description

Give your adult dog the nutrition they deserve with NutriPet Premium Adult Dog Food. Our carefully crafted chicken and rice formula provides complete and balanced nutrition for adult dogs of all breeds.

Made with real chicken as the first ingredient, this premium formula delivers high-quality protein to support strong muscles and overall health. The easily digestible rice provides essential carbohydrates for energy, while our blend of fruits and vegetables adds natural vitamins and antioxidants.

Key Benefits:

  • Supports healthy digestion with prebiotics and probiotics
  • Promotes a shiny coat and healthy skin with omega fatty acids
  • Maintains strong bones and teeth with calcium and phosphorus
  • Boosts immune system with antioxidants and vitamins
  • No corn, wheat, or soy - great for sensitive stomachs

Ingredients

Main Ingredients: Chicken, Chicken Meal, Brown Rice, Barley, Oatmeal, Chicken Fat (preserved with Mixed Tocopherols), Flaxseed, Natural Flavor, Dried Egg Product, Carrots, Sweet Potatoes, Peas

Feeding Guidelines

The following are general feeding recommendations. Adjust as needed to maintain optimal body condition.

Dog Weight Daily Amount

20-30 lbs 1.75 - 2.5 cups
